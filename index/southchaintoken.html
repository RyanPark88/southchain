SouthChain Token

The "SouthChain Token"(SCT) is the SouthChain Network native token. It serves several purposes in the operation of SouthChain Network it will be initially listed in Uniswap and Balancer Pools paired with Ethereum and trading fees will be earned for providing liquidity. All "SCT" holders are also granted Voting Rights in the DAO. We strongly encourage and hope all Token Holders take an active participation in the Governance of the Network.
